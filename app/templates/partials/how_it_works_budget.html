1. Draft Your Budget
Add line items for everything your department needs. Include quantities, costs, and notes to help reviewers understand your requests.
2. Review Period Starts
Once your request is ready, submit it for review. Your lines will go to the appropriate reviewer groups based on expense type.
3. Respond to Feedback
Reviewers may ask questions or request changes. Check back for "Info Requested" or "Changes Requested" items and respond promptly.
4. Final Approval
Relevant lines are sanity checked by the appropriate reviewer group(s), then admins complete the final review & approval. Once approved by an admin, your budget will be added to Divvy within 2 weeks or by July 15, whichever comes sooner.
After finalization: Your budget request becomes locked. If you need additional funds after your primary budget is finalized, you can create a supplementary request which follows the same review process.
