{# Workflow Reminders #}

Workflow Reminders

Review Process

  • AG APPROVED = Approved by Approval Group (Level 1)
  • APPROVED = Final Admin approval
  • Lines must go through AG review before Admin Final
  • You can override AG recommendations in Admin Final

Finalization

  • All kicked-back lines must be resolved first
  • Pending lines auto-approve at requested amount
  • A finalization note is required
  • Unfinalizing pauses related supplementary requests

Key Statuses

DRAFT Not yet submitted
SUBMITTED In review
NEEDS_INFO Waiting on requester
FINALIZED Fully approved
AG APPROVED Level 1 done
