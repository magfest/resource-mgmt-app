How the Budget Process Works
1. Draft Your Budget
Add line items for everything your department needs. Include quantities, costs, and notes to help reviewers understand your requests.
2. Submit for Review
Once your budget is ready, submit it. Your lines will go to the appropriate approval groups based on expense type.
3. Respond to Feedback
Reviewers may ask questions or request changes. Check back for "Needs Info" or "Needs Adjustment" items and respond promptly.
4. Final Approval
Lines are approved at the approval group level, then finalized by admins. Once finalized, your budget is locked and ready for the event.
Questions? Reach out to your division head or the budget team.
